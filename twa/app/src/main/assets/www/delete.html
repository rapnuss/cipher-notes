ciphernotes Account Deletion

Follow these steps:

  1. Open the App and click the menu button in the top right corner.
  2. Select "Delete Account".
  3. A 6 digit code will be sent to your email address.
  4. Enter the code and click "Delete Account".

Your account will be deleted and all your data will be deleted from the server.
