1. Service Description

Basic Service (Free)

  • Creation and management of offline notes and to-do lists with labels and colors (up to 1 MB of encrypted data, stored in Render/AWS, Frankfurt).
  • Import of notes from Google Keep.
  • Export function for notes.
  • Storage and display of files, images, and videos (up to 100 MB of encrypted data, stored in Wasabi Hot Cloud, Frankfurt).
    • Storage and synchronization are interrupted if the data volume exceeds the respective limits (1 MB for notes, 100 MB for files, images, and videos). Users receive an error message.
    • Users can delete content at any time to stay within the respective limits.
  • Synchronization of notes, files, images, and videos with the server and other devices (within the respective limits).
  • Use of hCaptcha to prevent automated registrations and login attempts.

Data Backup & Migration

  • Encrypted data (notes, files, images, videos) are stored for a maximum of 7 days for short-term backups.
